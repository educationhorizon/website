ABOUT US

Unlocking potential, unleashing excellence

Education Horizon is a transformative educational initiative dedicated to empowering every African child. We expand access to high-quality educational resources and opportunities, regardless of socio-economic background, because education is a fundamental right and a catalyst for personal growth, social progress and economic advancement.

We nurture curiosity, critical thinking and innovation through inclusive learning environments, forward-looking programmes, tailored learning experiences and strong community engagement. Our work equips children with the knowledge, confidence and practical skills they need to thrive in a complex and competitive world.

Our ambition reaches beyond the classroom: we seek to inspire a generation of leaders, thinkers and change-makers who will strengthen their communities and help shape a better future for Africa and beyond.

MISSION & VISION

Education that opens every horizon

Our Mission

To ensure equal access to quality education for every African child by creating inclusive, engaging learning environments that cultivate curiosity, creativity and intellectual growth, enabling every learner to realise their full potential and strive for excellence.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every African child has the knowledge, skills and resilience to succeed in an ever-evolving world. By fostering creativity and critical thinking, we aim to advance accessible education and sustainable progress across Africa and beyond.

OUR PROJECTS

Building foundations for lifelong success

Our strategic initiatives strengthen learning from early childhood through educator development and technology-enabled classrooms. Together they address educational disparities, promote inclusion and connect young people to wider opportunities.

Early Childhood Development

We strengthen ECD centres through educator training, inclusive and culturally responsive curricula, sound centre management and stimulating environments that build early literacy, numeracy, cognitive and social skills.

Teacher Development

Workshops, mentorship, practical teaching experience and curriculum enrichment support student teachers, graduates and in-service educators in Language, Mathematics and Science across Grades 1-7.

EduTech

Technology labs, hands-on STEAM learning, coding and robotics bring innovation into classrooms. We also train teachers to use digital tools effectively while helping bridge the digital divide.

Education Centre / Hub

Our long-term vision is a collaborative learning hub with modern STEAM facilities, robotics and science laboratories, a library and research centre, music and arts studios, and educator development resources.
